Projects:

ME_iON

ME_iON is a simple online multiplayer shooter where you battle as eather a watermellon or a mushroom that is tasked to take out as many of your opponents as you can, when you finish a multiplayer game in ME_iON you gather tokens that you can then use in your homestead, the homestead is an offline gamemode where you can use the tokens you have earned in online multiplayer to make a home away from home, you can also use these tokens to get cosmetic items ingame only through gameplay
